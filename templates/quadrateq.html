Quadratic equation has the following form

$$ a x ^2 + b x + c = 0,\ a \ne 0. $$

There are two main ways to solve quadratic equation:

  • 1. Using discriminant: \( x_{1,2}=\frac{-b \pm \sqrt{D}}{2a},\ D = b^2 - 4 a c,\ D > 0\).
  • 2. Using Vieta's formulas: \( x_1 \cdot x_2 = c / a,\ x_1 + x_2 = - b / a\).

If \(a = 1\) Vieta's formulas take the form: \( x_1 \cdot x_2 = c,\ x_1 + x_2 = - b\).
