Linear equation is the simplest equation and it has the following form

$$ a \cdot x = b $$

There are three possibilities:

  • 1. \(a \ne 0\). There is a unique solution: \( x = b/a. \)
  • 2. \(a = 0\) and \(b \ne 0\). There are no solutions: \(x \in \varnothing\).
  • 3. \(a = 0\) and \(b = 0\). There are infinitely many solutions: \( x \in R\).

Mainly there are equations of the first type. So, to solve such equation one needs to divide \(b\) by \(a\).
